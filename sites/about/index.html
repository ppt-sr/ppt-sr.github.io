ABOUT US!

About Us

Welcome to the official Poppy Playtime Speedrunning resource hub! We are the Poppy Playtime Speedrunning Mod Team, dedicated to curating and maintaining an extensive collection of glitches, strategies, skips, and guides discovered and shared by the community.


Ready to dive in?

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a centralized and accessible platform where speedrunners & glitch-hunters can easily find and contribute valuable resources. We aim to foster collaboration and push the boundaries of Poppy Playtime Speedrunning.


Your next world record could start here!

Behind the Scenes

We are more than just a collection of strategies and glitches — behind every guide, there’s a passionate team of moderators, speedrunners, and community members working together. From testing the latest skips to documenting key moments, we ensure every piece of content is thoroughly reviewed and verified.


We put in the work so you can focus on the run!

Get Involved

Have you found a new skip or strat? We encourage all community members to share their findings! Feel free to reach out to us on our Discord to contribute to the ever-evolving Poppy Playtime Speedrunning.
